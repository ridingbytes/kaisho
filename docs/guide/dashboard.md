# Dashboard

The dashboard is your home screen. It shows at a glance what needs
attention: running timers, hours tracked, open tasks, inbox items,
and budget status.

## Stat Cards

Four cards at the top summarize your current state:

| Card | Shows | Clicks through to |
|------|-------|-------------------|
| Open Tasks | Count of non-done tasks | Board |
| Inbox Items | Unprocessed captures | Inbox |
| Hours This Month | Total hours tracked | Clocks |
| Budgets at Risk | Customers over 80% budget | Customers |

Every number is clickable. This is a core principle in Kaisho: any
aggregated figure drills down into the entries behind it.

## Active Timer

When a timer is running, a banner shows the customer, description,
and elapsed time. Click **Stop** to end it, or click the timer to
edit the entry.

## Budget Status

The main section lists all active customers with their budget
consumption:

- **Green bar** -- under 80% of budget used
- **Orange bar** -- between 80% and 100%
- **Red bar** -- over budget

Click a customer row to expand it. You'll see:

- Individual clock entries for that customer
- Contract-level breakdowns (if the customer has contracts)
- Billable vs. non-billable split

Each expanded entry is editable inline: change the customer,
description, hours, or invoiced status without leaving the dashboard.

## Time Insights

A chart at the bottom shows your daily hours over the selected
period. Hover over a bar to see the breakdown by customer.
